Life @ My diet My life

At My diet My life, we get to the root cause of your weight problem by helping you tackle the reasons for your unhealthy eating behaviour. Our weight loss diet plans & management programs are personally supervised to help you lose weight quickly at a safe and sustainable rate. As you approach your goal weight, we’ll teach you how to maintain your healthy weight for life – so you can focus on living, not ‘dieting’
